# Privacy Policy for Salted Hoya's Games

Thank you for playing Salted Hoya's games! This Privacy Policy explains what information I collect and why I collect it. Your privacy is important to me, and I am committed to protecting it.

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to Salted Hoya's games. My core philosophy is to collect only the minimal, anonymous data necessary to improve the Game for everyone.

I **DO NOT** collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, IP address, or device identifiers.

## 2. What Information I Collect

I collect **anonymous, non-personal statistical data** about your gameplay. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you personally.

The anonymous data I collect includes:

- **In-game events:** Such as levels completed, characters used, items acquired, in-game achievements, etc.
- **Gameplay patterns:** Such as session length, feature usage, and other general gameplay behavior.
- **Game performance:** Such as crash reports and performance metrics to identify and fix bugs.

This information helps me understand how players interact with the Game.

## 3. How I Use Your Information

The anonymous data I collect is used **strictly for my internal purposes** to improve and balance the gameplay experience.

Specifically, I use this information to:

- **Balance the Game:** I analyze data to adjust difficulty, character abilities, item drops, and other game mechanics to make the game more fair and enjoyable.
- **Improve Features:** I identify which features are popular and which are not, helping me decide where to focus my development efforts.
- **Identify and Fix Bugs:** Crash reports and performance data help me find and resolve technical issues quickly.

The data will **NEVER** be used for advertising, marketing, or any purpose other than improving the Game.

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

I **DO NOT** sell, trade, or share the anonymous data I collect with any third parties, except where required by law. The information is for my internal use only.

## 5. Data Security

I am committed to protecting the security of the data I collect. I implement reasonable security measures to maintain the safety of this anonymous information.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. I will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the Game or on my website. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

## 7. Contact Me

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact me at: [https://x.com/saltedhoya](https://x.com/saltedhoya)
